# Simulation-of-AlGaAs-GaAs-Quantum-Well-LED-using-COMSOL
The simulation of a quantum well light-emitting diode (LED)  structure based on AlGaAs/GaAs using COMSOL Multiphysics. The objective is to  evaluate the electrical and optical behavior of the structure with M=4 quantum wells and  N=5 barriers. The structure includes n-type GaAs, quantum wells of GaAs, barriers of  AlGaAs and p-type GaAs.
